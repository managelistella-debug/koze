Common Questions

Before We Connect

No — the initial discovery call is complimentary. We want to learn about your project and determine if we're the right fit before either party invests significant time. There's no obligation and no pressure. If we're not the right match, we'll tell you honestly and point you in the right direction if we can.

We aim to respond within one business day. After receiving your inquiry, we'll reach out to schedule a discovery call at a time that works for you. We take every inquiry seriously — if you're a good fit for what we do, we want to move quickly to get the conversation started.

It helps to have a general sense of your goals, your budget range, your property address or target area, and your ideal timeline. Nothing needs to be finalized — the conversation is exploratory. We'll ask questions and listen carefully. The more honest you can be about your budget, the more useful we can be in return.

Our primary service area is the Lower Mainland of BC — from the Fraser Valley to the North Shore and Greater Vancouver. If your project is outside our listed areas, reach out anyway — we evaluate requests on a case-by-case basis. Distance isn't always a dealbreaker for the right project.

Our target minimum project budget is $500,000. We work with clients who understand that quality craftsmanship and a well-managed process represent a real investment — one that pays off over the life of a well-built home. Our average project is approximately $800,000–$1M. If you're unsure whether your budget qualifies, reach out and we'll have an honest conversation about what's achievable.
