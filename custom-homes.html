How We Do It Differently

Built on Three Principles

Every Koze custom home is built on a process designed to eliminate surprises and protect your investment from the first conversation to the final walkthrough.

01

Budget-Led Design

We discuss your goals and budget before a single line is drawn. Your plans are designed to fit your investment — not the other way around. No expensive revisions, no late-stage compromises.

02

Fixed-Price Contract

Scope is fully defined, materials are selected, and plans are finalized before you receive a price. One number. No change orders born from vague estimates. You know exactly what you're signing before we break ground.

Our Process

Three Phases. Complete Clarity.

A proven process that eliminates guesswork at every stage — from the very first conversation to the moment you receive your keys.

Phase One

Discover & Align

Budget, goals, and site review before design begins. We confirm the right fit through a questionnaire, discovery call, and in-person consultation — before any planning investment is made.

Phase Two

Design & Pre-Construction

Fully coordinated architecture, permits, interior design, and selections. Every decision is made before we price the work — so your fixed-price proposal reflects a fully defined scope.

Phase Three

Fixed Price & Build

One price. Full management. Turnkey delivery. We manage every trade, communicate proactively, and deliver exactly what we promised — on budget, on time.
