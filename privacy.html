Privacy Policy

Koze Design and Build Inc. (“Koze,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) is committed to protecting the personal information of our website visitors and clients. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard personal information in accordance with the Personal Information Protection Act (BC PIPA), PIPEDA (where applicable), and Canada’s Anti-Spam Legislation (CASL).

By using our website at kozedesignbuild.ca (the “Site”), you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. What Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you interact with the Site. This may include:

  • Contact information: Your name, email address, and phone number submitted through our contact or project inquiry forms;
  • Project information: Details about your property, project goals, budget range, and construction or renovation plans submitted as part of our qualification questionnaire or project inquiry process;
  • Communication records: Correspondence you send us via email, phone, or online forms;
  • Usage data: Non-personally identifiable information about how you interact with the Site, collected through cookies and analytics tools (see Section 6).

We do not collect sensitive personal information (such as financial account details or government identification numbers) through this Site. Any financial information required for project contracts is handled separately and securely outside of this website.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the personal information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Responding to inquiries: To review your project inquiry, assess project fit, and respond to your questions in a timely and helpful way;
  • Project intake and qualification: To evaluate whether your project goals and budget align with our services before scheduling consultations;
  • Project management and communication: If you proceed with Koze as a client, we use your information to manage the project relationship, coordinate design and construction milestones, and maintain clear communication;
  • Improving our services: To understand how prospective clients find us and what information is most useful, helping us improve our website and intake process;
  • Legal and contractual obligations: Where required to fulfil our legal, regulatory, or contractual responsibilities.

We will only use your personal information for the purpose for which it was collected, or for a directly related purpose. We will not use it in a way that you would not reasonably expect.

3. Disclosure of Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We do not share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: We may engage trusted third-party service providers (such as email hosting, website analytics, or project management software) who process information on our behalf under confidentiality obligations and only as necessary to provide those services;
  • Professional partners: If your project proceeds, relevant project details (not general personal information) may be shared with architectural, engineering, or design partners under non-disclosure agreements as part of coordinating your project;
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to a valid legal process, or if we believe in good faith that such disclosure is necessary to protect our rights or the safety of others.

4. Email Communications and CASL Compliance

Koze Design and Build Inc. complies with Canada’s Anti-Spam Legislation (CASL). We will only send you commercial electronic messages (such as project updates, newsletters, or promotional communications) if you have provided express or implied consent under CASL.

When you submit an inquiry or contact form on this Site, you are providing implied consent to receive communications directly related to your inquiry. If you wish to receive ongoing email communications of a promotional nature, we will ask for your express consent separately.

You may withdraw your consent to receive email communications at any time by:

  • Clicking the “Unsubscribe” link in any email we send you; or
  • Contacting us directly at info@kozedesignbuild.ca.

Please allow up to 10 business days for your withdrawal of consent to be processed. Withdrawal of consent does not affect the lawfulness of communications sent before the withdrawal.

5. How Long We Retain Your Information

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by applicable law. Inquiry information from prospective clients who do not proceed to project engagement is generally retained for up to two years. Client project records are retained for a minimum of seven years in accordance with applicable BC business and tax requirements.

When personal information is no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize it.

6. Cookies and Analytics

Our Site uses cookies and similar technologies to collect non-personally identifiable information about how visitors use the Site. This may include pages visited, time spent on the Site, and referral sources. This data helps us understand how our Site is performing and where we can improve the visitor experience.

We may use third-party analytics tools such as Google Analytics. These tools use their own cookies and are subject to the respective providers’ privacy policies. We do not use this data to personally identify individual visitors.

You may disable cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies will not significantly impair your ability to use this Site, though some features may be affected.

7. Security of Your Information

We take reasonable administrative, technical, and physical precautions to protect the personal information you provide from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. Our website uses HTTPS encryption for data transmission. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

We encourage you to avoid submitting highly sensitive information through web forms and to contact us directly by phone if your inquiry involves confidential details.

8. Your Rights Under BC PIPA

Under the Personal Information Protection Act of British Columbia, you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you;
  • Request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete personal information we hold about you;
  • Withdraw consent to the collection, use, or disclosure of your personal information (subject to legal and contractual restrictions);
  • Make a complaint about our privacy practices.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us in writing at the address below. We will respond to access and correction requests within 30 days, or notify you if an extension is required.

9. Children’s Privacy

This Site is not directed to children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you believe a child has submitted personal information to us, please contact us and we will take steps to remove it promptly.

10. Third-Party Links

Our Site may contain links to third-party websites (including social media platforms such as Facebook and Instagram). This Privacy Policy does not apply to those sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party site before providing personal information.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. The “Last updated” date at the top of this page indicates when the Policy was most recently revised. We encourage you to review this Policy periodically. Continued use of the Site following any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised Policy.
