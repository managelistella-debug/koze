Why We Exist

"The building process was broken. Budget surprises, missed timelines, and poor communication had become the accepted cost of building. They don't have to be."

Koze Design & Build was founded on a conviction that the best client experience and the best-built home are not competing priorities — they go hand in hand. Jason Craig built Koze after nearly a decade as a project manager watching homeowners navigate a process that was unnecessarily opaque, stressful, and expensive.

The problem wasn't the trades. It wasn't the materials. It was the process — or the lack of one. Budgets were set without real conversations about scope. Designs were created before anyone understood what was financially realistic. Clients were left managing multiple vendors with no single point of accountability.

Koze was built to solve this. Budget conversations happen before design begins. Scope is fully defined before a price is issued. One team handles everything — and one person is accountable for all of it.

Meet the Founder

Jason Craig

Founder & Lead Builder — Koze Design & Build

Jason didn't find construction — construction found him. He grew up watching his father develop properties across the Lower Mainland, and was working on job sites before he finished high school. By Grade 7 he was helping form foundations and performing labour work on active builds. By the time most of his peers were figuring out what they wanted to do, Jason already understood how a home came together from the ground up.

That hands-on foundation shaped everything that followed. After completing a Bachelor's Degree in Business, Jason returned to what he knew — but with a new perspective. He spent nearly a decade as a project manager, first supervising projects for his father's development company, then working alongside his brother's firm where he led a portfolio of construction projects across the Lower Mainland.

Nine years of project management gave Jason something rare in the trades: a deep, first-hand understanding of what clients actually experience. He saw what happens when communication breaks down, when budgets aren't anchored to reality, and when no single person is truly accountable for a project's outcome. He also saw what it looked like when everything worked — when a client was guided clearly, kept informed, and delivered exactly what they were promised.

"Building expertise alone isn't enough. Running projects professionally, communicating clearly, managing budgets honestly — those things matter just as much as what gets built. That's what Koze is built around."

Jason founded Koze in 2023 with a focused mandate: to build a design-build firm where the process is as good as the finished product. He is a husband and father, and he brings the same values he holds at home — honesty, long-term thinking, and accountability — to every client relationship and every project he takes on.

What We Believe

The Values Every Koze Project Is Built Around

Radical Transparency

You always know where your project stands, what it costs, and what comes next. No surprises, no silence, no chasing your builder for answers. We provide clear updates throughout every phase — because you deserve to know.

Budget First, Always

Design should follow your investment — not the other way around. Every Koze project begins with an honest budget conversation before a single plan is drawn. This protects your money, your time, and the relationship.

Built to Last

We select materials that can be repaired, maintained, and improved over time — not simply replaced. Hardwood over disposable flooring. Durable finishes over trendy ones. We build homes designed to age gracefully, not be replaced.

The Right Relationship

A custom home or major renovation lasts one to two years. We work only with clients where there's genuine alignment — on goals, budget, and communication style. Fit matters as much as capability.

One Point of Accountability

Architecture, interior design, permits, and construction — coordinated through one team and one process. You never manage multiple vendors or navigate competing priorities. One team from concept to completion.

Process Before Product

A great home starts with a great process. Ours is built around the moments where projects typically go wrong — budget alignment, communication, and scope definition — and designed to prevent each one.

Why Clients Choose Koze Over Other Builders

01
Budget-Led Design

Clarity Before the First Sketch

We discuss your goals and align on a realistic budget before design begins. Your plans are built around what's achievable — not scaled back after weeks of design work. Most builders design first and price later. We reverse the order because that's when it matters most.

02
Fixed-Price Contracts

You Know the Number Before We Break Ground

Scope, selections, and plans are fully defined before a contract is issued. You know exactly what your project costs before construction starts. No change orders driven by vague estimates — just one clear price for a clearly defined result.

03
Full-Service Design-Build

One Team. One Process. One Price.

We coordinate architecture, interior design, consultants, permits, and construction through our trusted network. You never manage multiple vendors or navigate competing timelines. One team is responsible for everything — from the first sketch to the final walkthrough.

04
Honest Qualification

Clarity First. Construction Second.

If your goals and budget don't align, we'll say so early. We'd rather lose a lead than build on unrealistic expectations. We qualify every project thoroughly — because a project that isn't the right fit isn't worth starting for either party.
