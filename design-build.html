Why Design-Build

The Problem With Managing Multiple Vendors.

Most homeowners are surprised to learn that managing a custom home or major renovation means coordinating between architects, interior designers, permit consultants, and contractors — often with conflicting timelines and incentives. When the architect's design exceeds the contractor's budget, the client absorbs the cost of revisions.

Koze eliminates this entirely. We act as the single point of coordination for every professional involved in your project. You have one contact. One schedule. One accountable team — from first sketch to final walkthrough.

  • Architectural design and coordination
  • Interior design — finishes, materials, fixtures
  • Permit preparation and management
  • Construction management
  • Consultant coordination (structural, geotechnical, energy)
  • Budget alignment at every phase

The Difference

Why Design-Build Delivers Better Outcomes.

In traditional construction, the architect designs what looks good on paper. The contractor then builds at the lowest cost to protect their margin. The client sits in the middle, managing conflicts between professionals who have never worked together before and have no shared financial stake in the outcome.

In our design-build process, design decisions are made with construction costs in mind from the start — because the same team is responsible for both. Costly surprises are caught in the design phase, not mid-frame. This alignment is why our budgets are more accurate and our projects more predictable.

When one team designs it and builds it, they design it to be buildable — and they build it as designed.

How It Works

Four Phases. Complete Clarity.

You'll know exactly where you are in the process — and exactly what comes next.

01
Discovery

Budget First. Design Second.

We start with your goals and your budget — not a design wishlist. A questionnaire and discovery call help us confirm alignment before any planning fees are invested. If your goals and your budget don't match, we tell you early.

02
Design

Plans Designed to Be Built — and Afforded.

Our architecture and interior design process unfolds in parallel with ongoing cost validation. Every design decision is reviewed against your budget — not just at the end, but at every major milestone. You approve the direction before it advances.

03
Pre-Construction

A Fixed Price Before Ground Breaks.

Once design is complete and permits are approved, we finalize material selections, coordinate all consultants, and prepare your fixed-price construction contract. You sign knowing exactly what your project costs — start to finish.

04
Build

Built as Designed. Delivered as Promised.

With a locked scope and a fixed price, our site team executes. We manage every trade, conduct regular inspections, and communicate proactively throughout the build. You'll always know where your project stands.
