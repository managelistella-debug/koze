About Koze Design & Build

Raised in Construction. Trained in Business. Built Through Experience.

Jason Craig grew up on construction sites — his father was a developer, and he was laying foundations before high school. After a business degree and nearly a decade as a project manager, he founded Koze on one belief: great building requires more than skilled hands. It takes clear communication, honest budgeting, and a process that puts clients first.

  • Budget-led design — aligned before a line is drawn
  • Fixed-price contracts before construction begins
  • One team: architecture, interiors, permits, build
  • Materials selected for longevity, not replacement

How It Works

Our Process

Three phases. Complete transparency. One fixed price before construction starts.

1

Discover & Align

We start with your budget and goals — not a design concept. A questionnaire and discovery call help us confirm we're the right fit before any planning investment begins.

2

Design & Pre-Construction

Our team coordinates architecture, interior design, permits, and consultants. You review plans, finalize selections, and receive a single fixed-price contract before we break ground.

3

Build & Deliver

With a locked price and finalized plans, construction begins. We manage every trade, communicate proactively, and deliver exactly what we promised.

FAQ
